Admission Process

Four Simple Steps to Join Our Family

We have designed a transparent and welcoming admission journey for parents and children.

01

Enquiry

Submit an online enquiry form or visit our campus to receive a guided tour and information pack.

02

Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents along with the registration fee.

03

Interaction

Friendly child-parent interaction with our admission team and academic counsellor.

04

Confirmation

Receive the offer letter, complete fee payment and welcome onboard!

Documents Required

What to Bring Along

Please carry the originals along with two sets of self-attested photocopies.

🪪

Identity & Age

Birth certificate (original & copy), four recent passport-size photographs of the child and one each of both parents.

🏠

Address & Family

Address proof (Aadhaar, utility bill or rental agreement), parents' Aadhaar / PAN, sibling certificate (if applicable).

📄

Previous School

Transfer certificate, the latest report card and conduct certificate. For Class IX onwards, board registration details.

🩺

Medical

Immunisation record, blood group certificate and any allergy / medical condition history.

💳

Registration Fee

Non-refundable registration fee paid online or by demand draft favouring "We Care English School".

✍️

Application Form

Duly filled and signed application form. Forms are available at the school reception or downloadable online.
