This folder is for your school's local images.

The current site uses Unsplash photo URLs as placeholders to keep the site self-contained and look professional out of the box.

When you're ready to use your own photography:
  1. Drop your image files into this folder.
  2. Open the HTML files and replace the Unsplash URLs (e.g., https://images.unsplash.com/...) with relative paths like images/your-photo.jpg.

Recommended sizes:
  - Hero slide images: 1800px wide
  - Section/card images: 800-1000px wide
  - Gallery thumbnails: 600px wide; full-size: 1400px wide
  - Photos should be JPG or WebP for best performance.
