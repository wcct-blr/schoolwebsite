Our Facilities

Home› Facilities
Campus Infrastructure

A Campus Built for Curious Minds

Spacious, safe and thoughtfully designed spaces that support every dimension of a child's growth — academic, creative, athletic and emotional.

Library

Reference & Reading Library

A cozy library with a carefully selected collection of books covering literature, science, biographies, academics, and general knowledge. Designed to encourage reading habits, quiet learning, and curiosity among students.

Science

Science Laboratories

Separate, fully-equipped Physics, Chemistry and Biology laboratories supporting SSLC practicals from Classes 5 to 10

Technology

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with 30 workstations,robotics kits and a maker-space for innovation projects.

Classrooms

Smart Classrooms

Every classroom is equipped with interactive smart boards, audio-visual aids and ergonomic furniture suited to each age group.

Sports

Playgrounds & Courts

Indoor space for badminton, table tennis, fitness and fun activities. Outdoor ground nearby for football, cricket and other sports

Health

Medical Room

An on-campus infirmary staffed by a panel doctor on call and eye and health screening for every child

Safety First

A Campus Where Every Child Feels Safe

Robust safety, security and wellbeing systems form the foundation of our daily operations.

🛡️

Prayer and meditation room

Prayer room for kids and teachers

📹

CCTV Coverage

Surveillance across classrooms, corridors, gates and bus pick-up points, monitored from a central control room.

🔥

Fire & Disaster Safety

Smoke detectors, fire extinguishers and well-marked evacuation routes. Mock drills are conducted every term.

💚

Counselling Support

A full-time school counsellor offers confidential emotional support to students and family workshops for parents.
