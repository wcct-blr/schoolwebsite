Our Programs

Learning at Every Stage

A continuous, joyful learning journey from the first steps in Kindergarten all the way to Senior Secondary specialisation.

Ages 3 – 6 · Nursery, LKG, UKG

Kindergarten

A vibrant, play-rich foundation where curiosity is sparked and confidence is built. Our Kindergarten program blends Montessori-inspired free play with structured early literacy and numeracy.

  • Theme-based activity learning
  • Phonics and early literacy through stories & songs
  • Number sense through manipulatives and games
  • Art, music, dance and rhythmic movement daily
  • Outdoor play, gardening and nature walks
  • Low teacher-to-student ratio for personal attention
Classes I – V · Ages 6 – 11

Primary School

The primary years build strong literacy, numeracy and inquiry skills. Our integrated, activity-driven curriculum aligns with the CBSE framework while preserving room for exploration and creativity.

  • Multilingual literacy: English, Hindi and Tamil
  • Activity-based mathematics with manipulatives
  • Inquiry-led EVS & introduction to science
  • Art, craft, music, dance and physical education
  • Continuous & Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE)
  • Library hour, computer class and weekly assemblies
Classes VI – VIII · Ages 11 – 14

Middle School

Middle school deepens understanding across the full CBSE curriculum, with a strong emphasis on scientific reasoning, written expression, and student leadership.

  • Full CBSE curriculum with Sanskrit as third language
  • Hands-on Physics, Chemistry and Biology lab work
  • Mathematics labs and problem-solving Olympiads
  • Coding fundamentals and digital literacy
  • Debate, public speaking and student council
  • Heritage clubs, eco-club and community service
Classes IX – XII · Ages 14 – 18

Secondary & Senior Secondary

Focused preparation for AISSE (Class X) and AISSCE (Class XII) examinations, with stream specialisation, career counselling and university guidance to launch every student towards their chosen future.

  • Science (PCM & PCB), Commerce and Humanities streams
  • Specialised subject electives and project work
  • Career counselling, aptitude testing & mentoring
  • Competitive exam coaching: JEE, NEET, CUET, CLAT
  • Internship and university guidance support
  • Leadership opportunities through prefects & council
